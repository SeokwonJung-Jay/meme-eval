This can include information such as the number of times the message has been dequeued, the time of the last dequeue attempt, and any error messages or exception details that were generated during processing.</p><p>Using dead-letter queue can be a good way to handle exception cases, like when there is an issue with a message or a service that is required to process the message, and it allows to separate the problematic messages from the normal ones and investigate them separately.</p></div>
